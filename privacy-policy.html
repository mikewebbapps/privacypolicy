Privacy Policy

✓ I do not collect, store, or share any personal data

Overview

My apps are designed with privacy as a core principle. I believe that your data belongs to you and you alone. This privacy policy explains my commitment to protecting your privacy.

What Data I Collect

I do not collect any user data.

My applications operate entirely on your device and do not:

  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Track your location or movement
  • Monitor your app usage or behavior
  • Send your data to external servers or third parties
  • Use analytics or crash reporting services that identify you
  • Display targeted advertising based on your data
  • Require account creation or login credentials

How Your Data is Protected

Since I don't collect any data, there is nothing for me to protect or misuse. All data you create within my apps remains entirely on your device. You have complete control over any information you generate while using my applications.

Third-Party Services

My apps do not integrate with third-party data collection services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. I don't share any information with external parties.

Children's Privacy

My apps do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone. I am fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR regulations regarding minors, as I collect no personal data from anyone.

Device Permissions

If my apps request permissions (such as access to photos, files, or other device features), these are only used locally within the app to provide the specific functionality you request. No data accessed through these permissions is transmitted elsewhere or stored on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of my apps following any changes means you accept the updated policy.
